Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior programming experience?

No prior programming experience is required for our beginner tracks. We start from the basics and gradually build up your skills. However, some familiarity with programming concepts will help you progress faster.

How long does it take to complete the curriculum?

The complete curriculum takes about 6 months if you dedicate 10-15 hours per week. However, you can go at your own pace, and many students start applying for jobs after 3-4 months of consistent learning.

Will I get a certificate upon completion?

Yes, Pro and Enterprise subscribers receive certificates for each completed course and a final certificate for the entire curriculum. These certificates can be shared on LinkedIn and other platforms.

Do you offer job placement assistance?

Enterprise subscribers receive comprehensive job placement assistance, including resume review, interview preparation, and connections with our hiring partners. Pro subscribers get access to our job board and networking events.
